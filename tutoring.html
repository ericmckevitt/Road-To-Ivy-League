Tutoring

Achieving your academic goals with some help along the way.

Online Tutoring

For $25 an hour, clients are paired with a tutor who is qualified in the specified area of study. Clients may request assistance on assignments, help with understanding course material, and any course-related guidance deemed necessary. Video-Chat services such as FaceTime or Zoom facilitate tutoring sessions. Recurring customers can request to have the same tutor as sessions can become more productive as the tutors learn each of their clients' learning styles. For the most productive use of time, it is recommended that clients use online tutoring for classes that do not involve dense mathematical notation as tutoring for these classes often presents a barrier in communcation.

In-Person Tutoring

For $45 an hour, clients are paired with a tutor who is qualified in the specified area of study. Clients may request assistance on assignments, help with undestanding course material, and any course-related guidance deemed necessary. Tutors will discuss and decide on a time and public meeting place for a scheduled tutoring session. Recurring customers can request to have the same tutor as sessions can become more productive as the tutors learn each of their clients' learning styles. In-Person tutoring sessions are especially effective in tutoring for math classes as the tutor and client are able to easily communicate mathematical functions on paper that are not easily communicated over the phone.

Personalized SAT Tutoring

For $50 an hour, clients are paired with tutors who have scored exceptionally well on the SAT test. If accessible, previous SAT or PSAT score breakdowns will help tutors understand the student's abilities in different subsections of the test. If the student has not yet taken either the SAT or PSAT, a free pretest will be offered to the client upon purchase of a tutoring session. Scores from the pretest will be analyzed in advance by the tutor to determine which areas should be prioritized in the client's unique tutoring session. Recurring customers will have a file of test scores which track the student's performance and improvement over time to better focus on exactly the tested areas that need attention.

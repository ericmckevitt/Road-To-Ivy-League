The Importance of a Good Schedule

It is critical that students understand how to build a schedule that both benefits their GPA and prepares them for the SAT and future college classes. Throughout high school, students will need to choose classes to fill their schedule each year. Though all AP classes are weighed equally when GPA is calculated, some AP classes are vastly different in difficulty from others. It is important that students understand which AP classes will be easiest, which provide the most utility for their specific needs, and which are managed better than others.
